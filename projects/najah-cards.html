Najah Association - Card Generator System

Description

  • Najah Association

  • 2024 - 2026

Developed an official branded contract card generator system using Laravel. The platform enables employees and family members to create customizable greeting cards for various occasions such as Eid celebrations and other special events.

The system features a fully manageable interface with drag-and-drop functionality, allowing users to create highly customizable cards with ease.
